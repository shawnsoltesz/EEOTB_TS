ABOUT US

Tampa Bay's Problem Worth Solving

The entrepreneurs of Tampa Bay have a lack of nurturing assistance available to them. There is also a lack of qualified talent to help businesses reach their goals. At Entrepreneur Empowerment of Tampa Bay, Inc., we listen to both sides of the community and build solutions based on their growing needs.

OUR ACCOMPLISHMENTS

Ambitious goals call for ambitious planning. In a very short period of time, Entrepreneur Empowerment of Tampa Bay, Inc. has accomplished setting up infrastructure and events that include:

  • Established an office within the heart of the Tampa Bay Community
  • Became members of the South Tampa Chamber of Commerce
  • Built a website for the general public to learn about Entrepreneur Empowerment of Tampa Bay, Inc.
  • Built a website with an investor focus
  • Created a meetup page for events that is continually adding active members.
  • Held first community meetup on November 16, 2001
